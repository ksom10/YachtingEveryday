About Us

Welcome to Yachting
Every Day!

At Yachting Every Day, we strive to provide an unparalleled boating experience that exceeds your expectations. Our custom 30′ 2020 Sea Chaser Center Console is the epitome of luxury on the water. With its spacious design and thoughtful amenities, including a convenient bathroom (head) for your comfort and an extended sun shade to shield you from the South Florida sun, every moment onboard is designed for your relaxation and enjoyment.

We understand that comfort is key during your boating adventure, which is why we've outfitted our boat with ample seating, complete with chairs and a table for your food and drinks. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a day out on the water, we ensure your comfort and convenience at every turn.

But it's not just about the amenities – it's about the experience. Captain Jesse Fitzpatrick, our USCG certified 100-Ton Master Captain, leads our excursions with a perfect blend of expertise and personality. Known for his professionalism, warm demeanor, and infectious sense of humor, Captain Fitzpatrick ensures that every trip with us is not only safe and enjoyable but also filled with genuine camaraderie and laughter. With years of experience and a dedication to excellence, Captain Fitzpatrick guarantees smooth sailing and unforgettable memories on the water.

Whether you're a seasoned sailor or a first-time boater, we invite you to join us on an adventure like no other. Contact us today to book your next boating excursion and experience the Yachting Every Day difference for yourself. Let's set sail together and make memories that will last a lifetime!
