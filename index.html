Yachting Every Day: Custom 30′ 2020 Sea Chaser Center Console

Our custom 30′ 2020 Sea Chaser Center Console offers a spacious and luxurious experience on the water. Equipped with a convenient bathroom (head) for your comfort and an extended sun shade over the entire back of the boat, you can enjoy relief from the South Florida sun. With ample seating provided by chairs and a table for your food and drinks, we ensure your comfort and convenience. Additionally, our boat features a Bluetooth radio system with speakers strategically placed around the boat, providing an immersive and enjoyable listening experience for all

Captain Jesse Fitzpatrick, our USCG certified 100-Ton Master Captain, is known not only for his expertise and professionalism but also for his personable nature and delightful sense of humor. With Captain Fitzpatrick at the helm, you'll experience not just a safe and enjoyable boating excursion but also genuine camaraderie and laughter. His extensive experience, dedication to excellence, and warm personality ensure smooth sailing and unforgettable memories on the water.
